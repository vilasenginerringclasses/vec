Courses Offered

Diploma

The Diploma in Engineering or Diploma in Technical Education is a program focused on practical and skills-oriented training. It is a technical course that only covers the essentials when ranked with an undergraduate engineering degree. Its duration is minimum 3 years.

Engineering

Engineering is a scientific field and job that involves taking our scientific understanding of the natural world and using it to invent, design, and build things to solve problems and achieve practical goals. We're surrounded by the work of engineers practically every moment of our lives.

BSC

Bachelor of Science (BSc) is an undergraduate degree course usually of three years duration. It is one of the most popular course choices among Science students after Class 12th.

11th & 12 th Science

Science is a systematic enterprise that builds and organizes knowledge in the form of testable explanations and predictions about the universe.
